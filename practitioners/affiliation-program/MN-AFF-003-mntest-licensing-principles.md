## Purpose

This document establishes the **governing principles** for MNTEST licensing.

It defines what MNTEST is, who may access it, and under what conditions.

Pricing and credit structures are defined separately in **MN-AFF-005 (MNTEST Pricing Schedule)**.

---

## WHAT MNTEST IS

MNTEST is:

- The official Multiple Natures assessment instrument
- A licensed tool, not a standalone product
- Proprietary to Multiple Natures International

MNTEST is **not**:

- A subscription service
- Included automatically with affiliation
- Transferable between practitioners
- Available to non-affiliated individuals

---

## CORE LICENSING RULE

**No affiliation → No MNTEST access.**

This rule is absolute and non-negotiable.

- Affiliation grants **eligibility** to license MNTEST
- MNTEST access is **licensed separately** from affiliation
- Affiliation does not include MNTEST credits or usage rights automatically

---

## ACCESS MODEL

### Eligibility

Only affiliated practitioners may:

- Purchase MNTEST credits
- Administer MNTEST assessments
- Receive and deliver MNTEST reports

### Pricing Model

MNTEST operates on a **pay-per-use** basis:

- No mandatory subscription
- Credits purchased in advance
- Volume discounts available
- Credits never expire

Specific pricing is defined in **MN-AFF-005 (MNTEST Pricing Schedule)**.

### No Subscription Required

Practitioners are not required to maintain ongoing MNTEST usage.

- Low-usage practitioners are not penalized
- No "use it or lose it" pressure
- Cost aligns with actual value delivered

---

## LICENSING CONDITIONS

### Revocability

MNTEST access may be revoked if:

- Affiliation lapses or is revoked
- MNTEST is materially misused
- Reports are altered, falsified, or misrepresented

Revocation of MNTEST access is separate from (but often coincides with) revocation of affiliation.

### Termination on Affiliation Lapse

If affiliation ends:

- MNTEST access terminates automatically
- Unused credits expire and cannot be refunded
- No new assessments may be administered
- Previously delivered reports remain valid as historical records

### Non-Transferability

MNTEST credits and access are:

- Tied to the affiliated practitioner
- Not transferable to other individuals
- Not resellable

### Audit Rights

Multiple Natures International reserves the right to:

- Monitor MNTEST usage patterns
- Verify practitioner affiliation status
- Investigate suspected misuse

---

## REPORT INTEGRITY

MNTEST reports:

- Must be delivered unaltered
- May not have language, framing, or posture changed
- May not be rebranded or white-labeled without explicit permission
- Must be presented as Multiple Natures reports

Practitioners may:

- Add their own commentary or interpretation
- Discuss reports with clients
- Use reports as part of broader services

Practitioners may not:

- Alter report content
- Remove MN branding
- Present reports as their own proprietary instrument
- Claim authorship of MNTEST methodology

---

## WHAT MNTEST LICENSING DOES NOT INCLUDE

MNTEST licensing does not include:

- Training on MNTEST administration (available separately)
- Certification as an MNTEST practitioner
- Endorsement of practitioner quality or methods
- Exclusive territory or client rights
- Marketing support or lead generation

MNTEST is a tool. How practitioners use it is their responsibility.

---

## RELATIONSHIP TO AFFILIATION

| | Affiliation | MNTEST Licensing |
|---|-------------|------------------|
| **What it governs** | Naming rights | Instrument access |
| **Fee structure** | Annual flat fee | Pay-per-use credits |
| **Required for the other** | Yes (MNTEST requires affiliation) | No (affiliation doesn't require MNTEST) |
| **Can exist alone** | Yes | No |

A practitioner may be:

- Affiliated without using MNTEST (valid)
- Using MNTEST without affiliation (not permitted)

---

## GOVERNING PRINCIPLE

MNTEST is an instrument, not a membership benefit.

Practitioners pay when they use it.
Affiliation is the gate; usage is the transaction.
