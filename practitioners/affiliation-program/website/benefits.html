What Affiliation Grants

As an affiliated practitioner, you receive specific, defined rights to use the Multiple Natures name in your professional practice.

What You Get

Naming Rights

Use "Multiple Natures" in your professional identity. Call yourself an "MN Practitioner" or "Multiple Natures Practitioner."

Directory Listing

Appear in the Official MN Affiliation Directory where clients and organizations can verify your current status.

MNTEST Eligibility

Access MNTEST, the official MN assessment instrument. (MNTEST credits licensed separately.)

Current Standing

Demonstrate that your MN status is current, verified, and maintained—not just historical.

What You Can Say

With active affiliation, you can use present-tense MN language:

  • "MN Practitioner"
  • "Multiple Natures Practitioner"
  • "I practice Multiple Natures"
  • "MN-based coaching / consulting / guidance"
  • Use the MN logo on your materials
  • Reference MN in your website, materials, and client work

What Affiliation Requires

As an affiliated practitioner, you agree to:

  • Maintain active affiliation (annual renewal)
  • Represent MN accurately
  • Not claim endorsement or authority beyond what affiliation grants
  • Not present MN as a diagnostic, prescriptive, or labeling system
  • Remove MN naming if your affiliation ends

What Affiliation Does NOT Include

The affiliation fee is for naming rights only. It does not include:

  • MNTEST credits (purchased separately)
  • Courses or training
  • Access to new materials or concepts
  • Certification
  • Endorsement of your services
  • Marketing support

Posture Requirements

For purposes of representation, MN must be presented as a way of seeing, not a tool for instruction or prescription. It should not be presented as:

  • A diagnostic system
  • A prescriptive method that tells people what to do
  • A personality typing system used to label or direct people
  • A decision-making framework

MNTEST Access

MNTEST is the official MN assessment instrument.

  • Affiliation grants eligibility to use MNTEST
  • MNTEST is licensed separately (pay-per-use credits)
  • No affiliation = no MNTEST access

MNTEST credits never expire while you're affiliated. If your affiliation lapses, unused credits are forfeited after 30 days.
