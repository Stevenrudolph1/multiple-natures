Installment Options

Annual affiliation can be paid in installments for convenience:

Payment Option Amount Total
Single payment €300 €300
2 installments (semi-annual) €160 × 2 €320

Important: Affiliation is always annual. Installments are a payment convenience, not a different product. You commit to the full annual fee when you choose an installment plan. Multi-year options require single payment.

What the Fee Includes

  • Permission to use the Multiple Natures name publicly
  • Listing in the Official Affiliation Directory
  • Eligibility to license MNTEST (licensed separately)
  • Recognition as a current MN Practitioner

What the Fee Does NOT Include

  • MNTEST credits or usage (purchased separately)
  • Training or courses
  • Access to new concepts or materials
  • Continuing education
  • Endorsement or certification
  • Marketing support

Affiliation is naming rights only.
