2. Definitions

"Affiliation" means the active, paid, time-bound license to use the Multiple Natures name, marks, and instruments as defined in these Terms.

"Certification" means historical completion of MN training, which is separate from and does not constitute Affiliation.

"MNTEST" means the official Multiple Natures assessment instrument, access to which requires active Affiliation and separate licensing.

"MN Marks" means the Multiple Natures name, logo, and associated branding elements.

"Affiliation Period" means the term for which Affiliation is granted, as specified in the Affiliation Pricing Schedule.

4.1 Accurate Representation

7.1 Grounds for Revocation

7.3 Effect of Revocation

Upon revocation:

  • All rights under these Terms terminate immediately
  • You must cease all use of MN Marks within 7 days
  • MNTEST access terminates; unused credits are forfeited
  • No refund of fees is provided

7.4 Scope of Revocation

Revocation affects representation rights only. It does not:

  • Erase your certification history (if any)
  • Restrict your ability to practice under another name
  • Restrict your use of MN ideas privately or descriptively
  • Constitute a judgment on your competence or character
