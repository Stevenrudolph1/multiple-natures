How to Apply

Becoming an MN Affiliated Practitioner is a straightforward process.

Application Steps

Step 1: Complete the Application

Fill out the application form with your details:

  • Full legal name (as it will appear in the directory)
  • Professional name (if different)
  • Email address (primary contact for notices)
  • Country and city/region
  • Website (optional, may be linked in directory)
  • Certification status (certified year / not certified)
  • Affiliation term (1-year, 2-year, or 3-year)

Step 2: Accept Terms & Conditions

Review and accept the Affiliation Terms & Conditions. These outline your rights and obligations as an affiliated practitioner.

Step 3: Pay the Affiliation Fee

Complete payment for your selected term. Annual affiliation can be paid in full or in installments (2 or 4 payments).

View pricing options →

Step 4: Activation

Upon payment confirmation:

  • Your affiliation is activated immediately
  • You receive a confirmation email with your start and end dates
  • You're added to the Official Affiliation Directory
  • MNTEST eligibility is activated

What Happens After Activation

Once affiliated, you can:

  • Update your website and materials with MN naming
  • Purchase MNTEST credits (separate licensing)
  • Share your directory listing with clients
  • Use the MN logo in your materials

Renewal

Affiliation renews annually. You'll receive reminders at:

  • 60 days before expiry
  • 30 days before expiry
  • 7 days before expiry

There is no auto-renewal—explicit action is required. You can renew up to 60 days early without losing time.

If Your Affiliation Lapses

If you don't renew by your expiry date:

  • Your affiliation lapses immediately (no grace period)
  • Directory listing is removed
  • MNTEST access is suspended
  • You must update materials to past-tense language

You can reinstate by paying the full annual fee. If you reinstate within 30 days, unused MNTEST credits are restored.
