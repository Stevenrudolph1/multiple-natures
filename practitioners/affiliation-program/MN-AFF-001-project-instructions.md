## Project Purpose

The MN Affiliation Project defines, establishes, and operationalizes a **clear boundary between free use of ideas and licensed use of the Multiple Natures name and instruments**.

This project does not govern thought, interpretation, or evolution of practice.
It governs **representation, naming rights, and access to official MN tools**.

Affiliation is a **current-status relationship**, not a reward for past training.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

**Ideas are free. Names are not.**

Anyone may think with, adapt, or move beyond MN concepts.
Only affiliated practitioners may present their work **as Multiple Natures**.

---

## PART A — GOVERNING LOGIC

### 1. What MN Affiliation Is

MN Affiliation is:

- An **active, paid, time-bound license**
- Permission to use the **Multiple Natures name and marks**
- A public signal of **current, verifiable status**
- Eligibility for licensed access to official MN instruments (licensed separately — see MN-AFF-003)

Affiliation exists to:

- Prevent misrepresentation
- Preserve clarity for the public
- Maintain coherence of the MN field over time

It does **not** certify quality, outcomes, or adherence to a method.

---

### 2. What MN Affiliation Is Not

Affiliation is not:

- Certification
- Endorsement
- Ownership of ideas
- Restriction on practice under another name
- A moral or community status

Affiliation confers **naming rights only**.

---

### 3. Certification vs Affiliation (Hard Separation)

This distinction is mandatory and irreversible.

- **Certification** = historical training record
- **Affiliation** = current permission to use the MN name and tools

A certified practitioner without affiliation:

- May state: "Trained in Multiple Natures (year)"
- May not:
  - Present services as MN
  - Use MN branding or naming
  - Use MNTEST
  - Claim current MN practitioner status

---

### 4. Grandfather Clause (Explicit)

Practitioners certified before the introduction of MN Affiliation:

- Retain their **certification history**
- Do **not** receive automatic or permanent naming rights

Affiliation is required for **ongoing public use of the MN name**, regardless of certification date.

---

### 5. Non-Affiliated Use: What Is Permitted

Non-affiliated individuals may:

- Reference MN as an influence
- State past training factually
- Use MN concepts privately or descriptively

**Permitted examples:**

- "Trained in Multiple Natures in 2019"
- "My work is informed by MN"
- "I studied with Steven Rudolph"

**Not permitted without affiliation:**

- "MN Practitioner"
- "Using Multiple Natures with clients"
- "Official MN approach"
- Any MN branding, logos, or tools

Non-affiliated use may not imply current standing, authorization, or official relationship.

This guidance must be published to reduce friction and ambiguity.

---

### 6. Revocation & Loss of Affiliation

Affiliation may be revoked or not renewed if:

- MN naming is used without active affiliation
- MN language or tools are materially misrepresented
- Branding or posture implies authority not granted

Revocation concerns **representation**, not practice quality or beliefs.

Loss of affiliation requires **immediate removal of MN naming**.
No retroactive penalties apply.

---

### 7. Enforcement Posture

Enforcement is:

- Quiet
- Factual
- Non-emotional

Process:

1. Private notice
2. Clear request to correct representation
3. Escalation only if ignored

No policing ideas.
No public shaming.
Only boundary maintenance.

---

### 8. Design Tone & Posture

All materials must:

- Avoid moral framing
- Avoid "community protection" rhetoric
- Avoid guru or authority signaling
- Emphasize clarity, choice, and transparency

The governing message:

> "You are free to do your work.
> If you want to do it *as Multiple Natures*, these are the terms."

---

## PART B — IMPLEMENTATION SCOPE

### 9. MNTEST — Governing Principles Only

MNTEST is:

- An official MN instrument
- Not a standalone or transferable product

Core rules:

- **No affiliation → no MNTEST access**
- Affiliation grants eligibility; MNTEST access is licensed separately
- MNTEST is pay-per-use, not included with affiliation

Pricing structure and terms are defined in **MN-AFF-005 (MNTEST Pricing Schedule)**.

MNTEST access must:

- Be revocable
- Be auditable
- Terminate automatically if affiliation lapses
