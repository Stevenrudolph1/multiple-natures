## Purpose

This document provides guidance for practitioners who want to create their own programs, courses, or offerings that are **informed by or incorporate Multiple Natures concepts**.

It clarifies:

- What you can create
- How you can describe it
- What language is permitted vs. prohibited
- The boundary between your work and official MN

---

## Core Principle

**You may build on MN. You may not certify as MN.**

Multiple Natures International encourages practitioners to innovate, adapt, and create. We do not control your creativity or restrict your practice.

We only govern:

- Use of the MN name
- Claims of MN authority
- Certification as MN

---

## What You CAN Do

### Create Your Own Programs

You may freely create:

✅ Courses informed by MN concepts
✅ Workshops that incorporate MN ideas
✅ Coaching programs that draw on MN
✅ Training for specific audiences (couples, teams, students, etc.)
✅ Niche applications of MN thinking
✅ Your own frameworks that build on MN

### Certify Your Own Programs

You may certify participants in **your** program:

✅ "Certified in [Your Program Name]"
✅ "Completed [Your Course] with [Your Name]"
✅ "Graduate of [Your Training]"

You are certifying **your work**, not MN.

### Reference MN as Influence

You may describe MN's role in your program:

✅ "Informed by Multiple Natures"
✅ "Incorporates MN concepts"
✅ "Draws on the Multiple Natures framework"
✅ "Built on foundations from MN"
✅ "Inspired by Steven Rudolph's work"

### Charge What You Want

You set your own pricing. MNI does not:

- Take royalties
- Require revenue share
- Approve your pricing
- Limit your business model

Your program is your business.

### Innovate Freely

You may:

✅ Adapt MN to new contexts
✅ Combine MN with other frameworks
✅ Develop your own terminology
✅ Evolve beyond MN
✅ Disagree with MN publicly

Ideas cannot be owned.

---

## What You CANNOT Do

### Certify Others as MN Practitioners

You may not:

❌ "Certified MN Practitioner"
❌ "MN Certification Program"
❌ "Official MN Training"
❌ "Authorized MN Trainer"
❌ "Licensed to certify MN practitioners"

**Only Multiple Natures International may certify or authorize certification as MN.**

### Imply Official MN Status

You may not suggest your program is official MN:

❌ "Official MN [anything]"
❌ "Authorized by Multiple Natures"
❌ "Endorsed by MN"
❌ "Approved MN curriculum"
❌ "MN-accredited"

### Use MN as the Primary Brand

You may not name your program as if it were MN:

❌ "MN Coaching Certification"
❌ "Multiple Natures Practitioner Training"
❌ "The MN Method Course"
❌ "MN Mastery Program"

Your program needs its own name. MN can be referenced as an influence, not as the brand.

### Grant MN Rights

You may not:

❌ Tell your graduates they are "MN Practitioners"
❌ Imply your certificate grants MN naming rights
❌ Suggest your training substitutes for MN affiliation
❌ Create tiers or levels of "MN certification"

## Naming Your Program: Examples

### ✅ Acceptable Names

| Program Name | Why It Works |
|--------------|--------------|
| "Strengths-Based Career Coaching (informed by MN)" | Your program, MN as influence |
| "The Clarity Method — drawing on Multiple Natures" | Your brand, MN referenced |
| "Couples Alignment Workshop" | Your program, no MN claim |
| "Leadership Coaching Certification by [Your Name]" | You're certifying your work |
| "Nature-Based Team Development" | Your terminology |

### ❌ Not Acceptable Names

| Program Name | Why It Fails |
|--------------|--------------|
| "MN Practitioner Certification" | Implies MN certification authority |
| "Official MN Couples Program" | Claims official status |
| "Certified MN Coach Training" | Certifies as MN |
| "Multiple Natures Mastery" | Uses MN as the brand |
| "Authorized MN Trainer Course" | Claims authorization |

## The Certification Boundary

This is the critical line:

| You Certify... | Permitted? |
|----------------|------------|
| Completion of YOUR program | ✅ Yes |
| Competence in YOUR methodology | ✅ Yes |
| Status as YOUR certified practitioner | ✅ Yes |
| Status as MN Practitioner | ❌ No |
| Competence in MN | ❌ No |
| Authority to use MN name | ❌ No |

**You certify your work. MN certifies MN.**

---

## What About Your Graduates?

### What They Can Say

If someone completes your MN-informed program, they may say:

✅ "Certified in [Your Program] by [Your Name]"
✅ "Trained in strengths-based coaching informed by MN"
✅ "Completed [Your Course]"

### What They Cannot Say

They may not say:

❌ "MN Practitioner" (unless they have MN affiliation)
❌ "Certified in Multiple Natures"
❌ "MN-certified coach"

Your certificate does not grant MN naming rights.

If they want to use the MN name, they need MN affiliation — separately, directly with MNI.

---

## Affiliation Requirement

### For You (Program Creator)

| Situation | Affiliation Required? |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Creating an MN-informed program | Recommended, not required |
| Referencing MN as an influence | No |
| Using MNTEST in your program | **Yes** |
| Calling yourself "MN Practitioner" | **Yes** |
| Marketing yourself as practicing MN | **Yes** |

If you're publicly presenting yourself as an MN Practitioner AND creating programs, you likely need affiliation.

If you're only referencing MN as an influence in your own branded work, affiliation is optional.

### For Your Graduates

Your graduates do not automatically become MN affiliated.

If they want MN affiliation, they apply directly to MNI — your program does not grant it.

---

## Using MNTEST in Your Programs

### If You Want to Include MNTEST

- You must be affiliated
- You must purchase MNTEST credits separately
- MNTEST reports must be delivered unaltered
- You may not rebrand MNTEST as your own instrument

### MNTEST Is Not Transferable

You cannot:

- Include MNTEST credits in your program fee and mark them up
- Resell MNTEST access to your students
- Create your own version of MNTEST

MNTEST remains a licensed MN instrument.

---

## Revenue and Royalties

### MNI Does Not Take Royalties

You keep 100% of your program revenue.

MNI does not:

- Charge licensing fees for MN-informed programs
- Require revenue share
- Approve your pricing
- Audit your income

### Your Only Costs

| If You... | You Pay... |
|-----------|------------|
| Reference MN as influence only | Nothing |
| Want to call yourself MN Practitioner | Affiliation fee (€300/year) |
| Want to use MNTEST | MNTEST credits (see pricing) |

---

## Why This Policy Exists

This policy exists to:

1. **Encourage innovation** — We want practitioners to build, adapt, create
2. **Maintain clarity** — The public should know what is MN vs. what is your work
3. **Protect the name** — "MN Practitioner" must mean something verifiable
4. **Stay out of your way** — We don't want to control your business

We are not a franchise. We are not taking royalties. We are not approving curricula.

We are simply maintaining one boundary: **who can certify as MN**.

## The Governing Message

> You are free to do your work. If you want to do it *as Multiple Natures*, these are the terms.

> Only Multiple Natures International may certify or authorize certification as Multiple Natures. Independent programs may certify their own curricula, even if informed by MN, but may not certify MN status.

**Ideas are free. Names are not.** We govern representation, not ideas, beliefs, or private practice.
