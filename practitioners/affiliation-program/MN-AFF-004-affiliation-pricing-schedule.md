## Purpose

This document defines the **current pricing** for MN Affiliation.

Pricing is separated from governing policy so it can be updated without revising core documents.

For what affiliation grants and requires, see **MN-AFF-001 (Project Instructions)**.

---

## AFFILIATION FEE

| Term | Single Payment | 2 Installments | 4 Installments |
|------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| 1 year | €300 | €160 × 2 (€320) | €85 × 4 (€340) |
| 2 years | €550 | — | — |
| 3 years | €750 | — | — |

**Installment payment:** Available for annual affiliation only. Multi-year options require single payment.

---

## WHAT THE FEE INCLUDES

The affiliation fee grants:

- Permission to use the Multiple Natures name publicly
- Listing in the Official Affiliation Directory
- Eligibility to license MNTEST (licensed separately)
- Recognition as a current MN Practitioner

---

## WHAT THE FEE DOES NOT INCLUDE

The affiliation fee does not include:

- MNTEST credits or usage (see MN-AFF-005)
- Training or courses
- Access to new concepts or materials
- Continuing education
- Endorsement or certification
- Marketing support

Affiliation is **naming rights only**.

## INSTALLMENT PAYMENT TERMS

| Term | Policy |
|------|--------|
| **What it is** | Annual affiliation, payable in installments |
| **What it is not** | Monthly subscription or membership |
| **Commitment** | Full annual amount committed at sign-up |
| **Options** | 2 payments (semi-annual) or 4 payments (quarterly) |
| **Premium** | Installment plans slightly higher than single payment |
| **Missed payment** | Affiliation lapses immediately — no grace period |
| **Reinstatement after missed payment** | Full annual fee required |
| **Multi-year installments** | Not available — multi-year requires single payment |

**Important:** Affiliation is always annual. Installments are a payment convenience, not a status option. You are committing to the full year when you choose an installment plan.

## RENEWAL

| Term | Policy |
|------|--------|

| **Auto-renewal** | No — explicit renewal required |
| **Lapsed affiliation** | Immediate removal from directory; naming rights suspended |
| **Reinstatement** | Full fee required; no pro-rating |
