## 4. PRACTITIONER OBLIGATIONS

### 4.1 Accurate Representation

You agree to:

a) Represent MN accurately and without material distortion

b) Distinguish your personal methods and opinions from MN itself

c) Not claim endorsement, authorization, or approval beyond what Affiliation grants

For purposes of representation and public description only, you agree that MN will not be presented as:

a) A diagnostic system

### 4.3 MNTEST Obligations

## 6. TERM AND RENEWAL

This structure follows standard professional practice: a law degree is permanent, but bar membership requires annual renewal to practice; a medical degree is permanent, but licensure requires maintenance. Certification records competence. Affiliation grants current permission to represent.

### 6.1 Affiliation Period

a) Affiliation is granted for the period specified at purchase (typically one year)

b) Affiliation begins upon payment confirmation

c) Affiliation expires automatically at the end of the Affiliation Period

### 6.2 Renewal

a) MNI will provide renewal notice at least 30 days before expiry

b) Renewal requires explicit action and payment; there is no auto-renewal

c) Failure to renew results in immediate lapse of Affiliation

### 6.3 Lapse

Upon lapse of Affiliation:

a) All rights to use MN Marks terminate immediately

b) You must remove MN naming from all public materials within 14 days

c) Directory listing is removed

d) MNTEST access terminates; unused credits expire

e) You may continue to reference past training factually (see Section 8)

## 7. REVOCATION

### 7.1 Grounds for Revocation

MNI may revoke Affiliation if you:

a) Use MN Marks in ways that violate these Terms

b) Materially misrepresent MN, its posture, or its instruments

c) Imply endorsement, authority, or official status not granted

d) Fail to correct representation issues after notice

e) Engage in public misrepresentation that materially harms the reputation of MN (this concerns representation only, not private opinions, beliefs, or critiques of MN philosophy)

### 7.2 Revocation Process

a) MNI will provide written notice specifying the grounds for revocation

b) You will have 14 days to respond or correct the issue

c) If not resolved, revocation becomes effective immediately

d) Revocation decisions are at MNI's sole discretion

### 7.3 Effect of Revocation

Upon revocation:

a) All rights under these Terms terminate immediately

b) You must cease all use of MN Marks within 7 days

c) MNTEST access terminates; unused credits expire

d) No refund of fees is provided

e) You may not reapply for Affiliation for 12 months unless otherwise agreed

### 7.4 Scope of Revocation

Revocation affects representation rights only. Certification is NEVER revoked — it is a permanent historical record of training you completed.

Revocation does not:

a) Erase your certification history (certification can never be taken away)

b) Restrict your ability to practice under another name

c) Restrict your use of MN ideas privately or descriptively

d) Constitute a judgment on your competence or character
