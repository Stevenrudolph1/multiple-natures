## Purpose

This document defines terms for **institutional and enterprise licensing** — when an organization (not an individual) wants to:

- Affiliate multiple practitioners
- Purchase MNTEST credits in bulk
- Use MN within an organizational context

---

## Who This Applies To

This document applies to:

- Schools and educational institutions
- Training organizations
- Corporations and businesses
- Consulting firms
- Non-profits and NGOs
- Government agencies
- Any entity (not individual) seeking MN licensing

---

## PART A — INSTITUTIONAL AFFILIATION

### Core Principle

**Affiliation is individual, not organizational.**

An organization cannot "be affiliated." Only individual practitioners can hold affiliation.

However, organizations can:

- Sponsor affiliation for their staff/members
- Manage multiple affiliations administratively
- Receive consolidated billing

### Sponsored Affiliation

An organization may pay for and manage affiliations on behalf of individuals.

| Feature | Policy |
|---------|--------|
| Who holds affiliation | The individual practitioner |
| Who pays | The organization |
| Who manages renewal | The organization (or individual, as agreed) |
| Directory listing | Individual names (not organization name) |
| Transferability | No — if individual leaves, affiliation stays with them or lapses |

### Sponsored Affiliation Pricing

| Number of Practitioners | Per-Person Annual Fee | Total |
|------------------------|----------------------|-------|
| 1–4 | €300 | Standard pricing |
| 5–9 | €270 | 10% discount |
| 10–19 | €250 | 17% discount |
| 20–49 | €225 | 25% discount |
| 50+ | €200 | 33% discount |

Volume discounts apply to new affiliations paid together. Renewals at the same rate if volume maintained.

### Administrative Features for Institutions

Organizations sponsoring 5+ affiliations receive:

- Single invoice for all affiliations
- Consolidated renewal management
- Designated administrative contact
- Bulk renewal reminders (to admin, not individuals)

### What Institutional Sponsorship Does NOT Include

- Organization-level MN branding rights
- Right to certify practitioners as MN
- MNTEST credits (purchased separately)
- Curriculum approval or endorsement
- Exclusive territory

## PART B — INSTITUTIONAL MNTEST ACCESS

### Core Principle

**MNTEST access requires individual affiliation.**

Organizations cannot hold MNTEST credits at the organizational level. Credits are tied to affiliated individuals.

However, organizations can:

- Purchase credits in bulk for distribution
- Manage credit allocation administratively
- Receive volume pricing

### Institutional MNTEST Pricing

| Credits | Price per Test | Total |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| 1–49 | Standard rates | See MN-AFF-005 |
| 50–99 | €24 | Volume rate |
| 100–249 | €22 | Institutional rate |
| 250–499 | €20 | Enterprise rate |
| 500+ | €18 | Custom agreement |

### Credit Allocation

Organizations purchasing bulk credits may:

- Allocate credits to specific affiliated practitioners
- Reallocate unused credits between affiliated staff
- Track usage by practitioner

Organizations may NOT:

- Hold credits without affiliated practitioners to use them
- Transfer credits to non-affiliated individuals
- Resell credits externally

### Credit Policies (Institutional)

| Policy | Institutional Terms |
|--------|---------------------|
| Expiry | Credits never expire (while organization maintains affiliated staff) |
| Expiry on lapse | Credits expire if no affiliated practitioners remain |
| Refunds | Non-refundable |
| Reallocation | Permitted between affiliated staff within the organization |

## PART C — ORGANIZATIONAL USE OF MN

### Can an Organization Use the MN Name?

**Limited, with conditions.**

An organization may say:

✅ "Our team includes MN-affiliated practitioners"
✅ "We use Multiple Natures in our work"
✅ "[X] of our staff are MN Practitioners"
✅ "MN-informed services available"

An organization may NOT say:

❌ "We are an MN-certified organization"
❌ "Official MN training provider"
❌ "MN-accredited institution"
❌ "Authorized MN partner"

### Organizational Branding

Organizations may NOT use:

- MN logo on organizational materials (without explicit written permission)
- "Official," "Authorized," "Accredited" in relation to MN
- MN as the primary brand for their services

Individual affiliated practitioners within the organization may use MN naming per standard affiliation terms.

### Co-Branding Requests

Organizations seeking co-branding, partnership designation, or logo usage must request explicit written permission from MNI.

Such arrangements are:

- Rare
- Case-by-case
- Not guaranteed
- Subject to separate agreement

## PART D — TRAINING ORGANIZATIONS

### Organizations That Train Others

If your organization trains practitioners and wants to reference MN:

| You Want To... | Permitted? | Requirements |
|----------------|------------|--------------|
| Train people in MN-informed methods | ✅ Yes | None |
| Reference MN as influence in your curriculum | ✅ Yes | None |
| Certify graduates in YOUR program | ✅ Yes | None |
| Certify graduates as "MN Practitioners" | ❌ No | Not permitted |
| Call yourself "Official MN Training Provider" | ❌ No | Not permitted |
| Use MNTEST in your training | ✅ Yes | Affiliation + credits required |

Full guidance: [Independent Programs Guidance (MN-AFF-012)](#)

### Graduates of Institutional Training

If your organization trains people in MN-informed methods:

- Your graduates receive YOUR certification, not MN certification
- They may NOT call themselves "MN Practitioners" based on your training
- If they want MN affiliation, they apply individually to MNI

Your certificate ≠ MN affiliation.

## PART E — SCHOOLS AND EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS

### Schools Using MN

Educational institutions (K-12, universities, etc.) may:

✅ Use MN concepts in curriculum
✅ Have affiliated staff deliver MN-informed instruction
✅ Use MNTEST with students (with appropriate consent)
✅ Reference MN in program descriptions

Educational institutions may NOT:

❌ Grant students "MN Practitioner" status
❌ Claim "MN-accredited" program status
❌ Use MN logo without permission

### Student MNTEST Usage

When using MNTEST with students:

- Affiliated practitioner must administer
- Appropriate consent/assent required (especially for minors)
- Reports delivered per standard MNTEST policies
- No bulk discount beyond institutional pricing above

### Academic Research

Researchers studying MN may request:

- Access to MN materials for research purposes
- MNTEST access for research (subject to ethics approval)
- Permission to publish findings

## PART F — ENTERPRISE / CORPORATE USE

### Corporations Using MN Internally

Companies may:

✅ Use MN for internal team development
✅ Have affiliated staff deliver MN-informed coaching/training
✅ Use MNTEST for employee development
✅ Reference MN in internal communications

Companies may NOT:

❌ Claim "MN-certified company" status
❌ Use MN logo on corporate materials without permission
❌ Offer "MN services" to external clients without affiliated practitioners

### External Client Work

If a company offers MN-informed services to external clients:

- Individual practitioners serving clients must be affiliated
- Company may describe services as "MN-informed" if delivered by affiliated staff
- Company may NOT claim organizational MN status

## PART G — AGREEMENT STRUCTURE

### How Institutional Licensing Works

1. **Inquiry** — Organization contacts MNI
2. **Needs Assessment** — MNI determines scope (# practitioners, MNTEST volume)
3. **Proposal** — MNI provides pricing and terms
4. **Agreement** — Organization signs institutional agreement
5. **Activation** — Individual affiliations activated; credits allocated
6. **Management** — Ongoing admin via designated contact

### Institutional Agreement Includes

- Number of sponsored affiliations
- MNTEST credit allocation (if applicable)
- Billing terms
- Administrative contact designation
- Renewal terms
- Termination provisions

### Agreement Term

- Minimum 1 year
- Renewable annually
- 60 days notice for non-renewal

---

## PART H — TERMINATION

### If Organization Ends Agreement

- Individual practitioners may continue affiliation at personal expense
- Or affiliations lapse per standard terms
- Unused MNTEST credits expire and cannot be refunded
- No ongoing obligations for organization

### If Individual Leaves Organization

- Their affiliation remains theirs (if they continue paying)
- Or lapses if organization stops sponsoring and individual doesn't take over
- Credits allocated to that individual may be reallocated (if unused)
