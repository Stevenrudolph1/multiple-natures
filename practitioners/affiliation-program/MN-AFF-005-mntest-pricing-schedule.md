## Purpose

This document defines the **current pricing** for MNTEST credits.

Pricing is separated from licensing principles so it can be updated without revising core documents.

For licensing rules and conditions, see **MN-AFF-003 (MNTEST Licensing Principles)**.

---

## PRICING PHILOSOPHY

MNTEST pricing reflects its role as a **working instrument** within an affiliation-based system.

- Affiliation (MN-AFF-004) carries the legitimacy and naming-rights cost
- MNTEST pricing covers tool value, delivery cost, and fair margin
- Pricing encourages regular, habitual use — not hoarding or hesitation

---

## PRICING MODEL

MNTEST operates on a **pay-per-use** basis.

| Principle | Policy |
|-----------|--------|
| **Subscription required** | No |
| **Prepaid credits** | Yes |
| **Credits expire** | No — credits never expire |
| **Volume discounts** | Yes |

## CREDIT PRICING

| Credits | Price per Test | Total Price | Savings |
|---------|----------------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | €40 | €40 | — |
| 5 | €35 | €175 | 13% |
| 20 | €30 | €600 | 25% |

| 100+ | €24 | Custom invoice | 40% |

**Working center:** €30–35 per test (typical practitioner experience)

**Floor:** €24 (high-volume users, institutions)

**Ceiling:** €40 (single-test purchases)

---

## WHAT A CREDIT INCLUDES

One MNTEST credit includes:

- One full MNTEST assessment administration
- One complete MNTEST report

---

## WHAT A CREDIT DOES NOT INCLUDE

Credits do not include:

- Training on MNTEST interpretation
- Client consultation time
- Follow-up assessments (each requires a new credit)
- Bulk report generation tools
- White-labeling or rebranding rights

## PURCHASE TERMS

| Term | Policy |
|------|--------|
| **Eligibility** | Affiliated practitioners only |
| **Payment** | Prepaid; credits issued upon payment confirmation |
| **Currency** | EUR (or local equivalent at published rate) |
| **Invoicing** | Available for purchases of 20+ credits |
| **Refunds** | Non-refundable once credits are issued |

---

## CREDIT POLICIES

| Policy | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Expiry** | None — credits never expire |
| **Transferability** | Not transferable between practitioners |
| **Resale** | Prohibited |
| **Forfeiture** | Unused credits forfeited if affiliation lapses |

---

## USAGE TRACKING

| Feature | Policy |
|---------|--------|
| **Balance visibility** | Practitioners can view remaining credits |
| **Usage history** | Available for audit and record-keeping |
| **Low balance alerts** | Optional notification when credits are low |

---

## AFFILIATION REQUIREMENT

| Condition | MNTEST Access |
|-----------|---------------|
| Active affiliation | ✓ May purchase and use credits |
| Lapsed affiliation | ✗ No new purchases; unused credits forfeited |
| Revoked affiliation | ✗ Immediate termination; credits forfeited |

MNTEST access requires active affiliation. See **MN-AFF-004 (Affiliation Pricing Schedule)** for affiliation terms.
