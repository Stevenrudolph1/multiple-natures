## Why

Theory was scattered: MN theory was here, Renergence theory was in `renOS/canonical/`, A/S/P was inside `renOS/canonical/{alignment,structure,positioning}/`. New readers (and Claude) had to know to look in three unrelated subdirs to assemble the foundational picture.

The `theory/` folder gathers them. See `~/Projects/theory/README.md`.

## Status

Corralling, not locked. Files may rename, subfolders may merge or split. The move is to make theory navigable, not to freeze it.
