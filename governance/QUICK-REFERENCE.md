# MN Governance — Quick Reference

## Can I use MN for...?

### ✅ YES (Permitted)
- Personal orientation and self-exploration
- Professional coaching/consulting (with training)
- Academic research and citation
- Career exploration support

### ⚠️ REQUIRES LICENSE
- Commercial/organizational integration
- MN-branded tools or programs
- Derivative assessments
- Training programs at scale

### ❌ NO (Prohibited)
- Identity assignment ("you are a ___ type")
- Outcome prediction ("you will succeed in...")
- Action prescription ("given your natures, you should...")
- Hiring/firing decisions
- Clinical diagnosis
- Systems that override person's agency

## What MNTEST Can/Cannot Tell You

### ✅ CAN
- Patterns in MI/MN expression
- What tends to energize/drain
- Context for friction
- Vocabulary for differences

### ❌ CANNOT
- What job you should take
- Whether you'll succeed
- Who you are as a person
- Your destiny
- Clinical information

## Supreme Constraint

**No MN system may outrank the person it serves.**

## Where MN Stops

**MN stops at orientation.**

✅ Observation: "This pattern seems present"
❌ Authority: "This pattern means you should..."

## When in Doubt

If unsure whether use is appropriate:
1. Does it assign identity? → ❌ Stop
2. Does it prescribe action? → ❌ Stop
3. Does it predict outcomes? → ❌ Stop
4. Can person set it down? → If no, ❌ Stop
5. Does it observe patterns? → ✅ Likely appropriate
