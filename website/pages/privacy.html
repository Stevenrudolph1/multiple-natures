Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

Last updated: January 2026

This privacy policy describes how Multiple Natures collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and services.

Information we collect

Information you provide. When you contact us, apply for training or affiliation, or take the MN Mapper (formerly MNTEST), you provide information such as your name, email address, and responses to assessment questions. We collect this information only when you choose to provide it.

Automatically collected information. When you visit our website, we may collect standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This information helps us understand how people use our site and improve its functionality.

How we use information

To provide services. We use the information you provide to deliver the services you request—responding to inquiries, processing training or affiliation applications, and providing MN Mapper results.

To improve our services. We may use aggregated, anonymized data to understand patterns in how people use our services and to improve them.

To communicate with you. We may use your contact information to respond to your inquiries or to send information relevant to services you have requested.

Information sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

Service providers. We may share information with service providers who help us operate our website and services, under confidentiality agreements.

Legal requirements. We may disclose information if required by law or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others.

With your consent. We may share information when you have given us explicit permission to do so.

MN Mapper data

The MN Mapper collects responses to assessment questions. This data is used to generate your results. We treat assessment data with particular care:

Your individual MN Mapper responses and results are confidential. We do not share them with anyone without your explicit consent.

Aggregated, anonymized assessment data may be used for research and framework development. This data cannot be traced back to individuals.

Data security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please contact us.
