Investment

Training investment covers the complete cohort experience: all live sessions, materials, between-session work, and feedback throughout the program.

Specific pricing will be listed with each scheduled cohort. Payment plans may be available.

Training investment is separate from affiliation fees. Completing training makes you eligible for affiliation; affiliation has its own annual fee.

What's included

All scheduled live sessions for the cohort duration.

Training materials and resources.

Between-session work and assignments.

Feedback and correction throughout the program.

Eligibility to apply for MN Affiliation upon completion.

Enrollment process

When a cohort is open for enrollment:

1. Review prerequisites. See who training is for to confirm this is the right fit.

2. Complete application. Provide information about your professional context and interest in MN.

3. Receive confirmation. Applications are reviewed and confirmed based on fit and cohort capacity.

4. Complete payment. Secure your place in the cohort.

5. Receive onboarding. Materials and preparation instructions before the first session.
