Affiliation Pricing

Annual affiliation costs, what's included, MN Mapper licensing, payment terms, and renewal.

Annual affiliation fee

Affiliation costs $XXX per year.

This is the standard rate for individual practitioners. The fee is the same whether you are affiliating for the first time or renewing.

The fee covers your affiliation year, which runs from your approval date (or renewal date) for twelve months.

What's included

The annual affiliation fee includes:

Naming rights. Permission to use Multiple Natures language in the present tense. "I am an MN Practitioner," "I practice MN," and similar statements.

Directory listing. Your entry in the Practitioner Directory, making you findable by people seeking MN practitioners.

MN Mapper eligibility. The right to apply for MN Mapper licensing (the license itself is separate).

Community access. Connection to other practitioners and access to shared resources.

Ongoing updates. Updates as the framework evolves, including refinements to theory and practice guidance.

MN Mapper licensing

MN Mapper licensing is separate from affiliation.

Affiliation makes you eligible to license the MN Mapper. The license itself has its own terms and costs.

If you want to use the MN Mapper with clients, you need both: affiliation (which costs $XXX/year) and an MN Mapper license (which has separate pricing based on usage).

For MN Mapper licensing details, see the MN Mapper section or contact us.

Payment terms

The affiliation fee is due at the time of application or renewal.

Payment is required before your application is processed. Your affiliation does not begin until payment is received.

We accept major credit cards and bank transfers. For alternative payment arrangements, contact us.

The fee is non-refundable once your affiliation begins. If your application is not approved, the fee is refunded in full.

Renewal process

Affiliation runs for one year and must be renewed to continue.

Before your affiliation year ends, you will receive a renewal notice. The notice is sent approximately 30 days before expiration.

To renew, you complete a brief renewal form and pay the current annual fee. There is no re-application process; renewal is simpler than initial application.

If you renew before your current affiliation expires, your new year begins when the current one ends. There is no gap and no lost time.

If you renew after your affiliation has already expired, your new year begins from the date of renewal. There is no retroactive coverage for the lapsed period.

What happens if affiliation lapses

If you do not renew and your affiliation expires, several things change:

Your naming rights end. You can no longer use present-tense MN language ("I am an MN Practitioner").

Your directory listing is removed.

Your MN Mapper license (if you have one) becomes inactive.

You retain the right to speak about your training in past tense. "I completed MN training" remains accurate.

There is no penalty for letting affiliation lapse. You can re-affiliate at any time by completing the application process and paying the current fee.
