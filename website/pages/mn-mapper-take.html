Use the MN Mapper

(formerly MNTEST)

Surface patterns in your engagement. A starting point for observation.

What to expect

The MN Mapper takes approximately 20-30 minutes to complete. You will respond to scenarios and statements that surface patterns in how you engage with different kinds of situational demands.

There are no right or wrong answers. The test is designed to reveal what flows easily for you and what carries more cost—not to judge or evaluate your capabilities.

Your results will show patterns across the Nine Natures and the Ten Intelligences. These results are a starting point for observation, not a final determination of who you are.

Before you begin

Answer based on how you actually respond, not how you think you should respond. The test surfaces what is, not what you wish were true.

There is no preparation needed. Your first responses are usually the most accurate.

Find a quiet moment when you can focus. The test works best when you can give it your attention without interruption.

After you complete the test

Your results will be available immediately. Take time to sit with them before drawing conclusions.

For guidance on understanding your results, see how to interpret your results.

Remember that results are a starting point. Continue to observe how the patterns show up in your actual life. Verify, adjust, and refine over time.

Individual vs. Professional versions

The MN Mapper is available in two versions:

Individual MN Mapper. For personal use. Available to anyone who wants to explore their own patterns.

Professional MN Mapper. For use with clients. Available to affiliated practitioners who have completed the separate MN Mapper licensing process.

If you are a professional interested in using the MN Mapper with clients, see the Affiliation Program for the pathway to professional access.

Privacy

Your responses and results are handled according to our privacy policy. We do not share individual results without your explicit consent.
