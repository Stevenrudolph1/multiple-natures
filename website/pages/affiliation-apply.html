How to Apply for Affiliation

The application process for becoming an MN affiliate. Prerequisites, steps, timeline, and what to expect.

Prerequisites

Before applying for affiliation, you must have completed MN Training.

Training is where you learn the framework. Affiliation is where you practice it. The sequence matters: you cannot affiliate without first being trained.

If you completed training through an earlier program or under a different name, that counts. The requirement is completion of the training, not when or under what name it was offered.

If you have not yet completed training, start with MN Training.

Application steps

Step 1: Complete the application form. The form asks for basic information about you, your training history, and how you intend to use MN in your practice. It takes about 15 minutes to complete.

Step 2: Submit training documentation. Provide verification of your training completion. This is typically your certificate or confirmation from the training program. If you cannot locate your documentation, contact us to discuss alternatives.

Step 3: Pay the affiliation fee. The annual affiliation fee is due at the time of application. See pricing for current amounts and payment options.

Step 4: Wait for review. Applications are reviewed within two weeks of submission. You will receive confirmation of receipt and a decision by email.

Review timeline

Applications are reviewed in the order received. Most are processed within two weeks.

The review confirms your training completion and ensures the application is complete. It is not an evaluation of your competence or practice quality.

If additional information is needed, you will be contacted. This may extend the timeline.

After approval

If your application is approved, several things happen:

Your affiliation begins. Your affiliation year starts from the date of approval. It runs for one year and requires renewal before it expires.

You receive confirmation. You will receive written confirmation of your affiliate status, including your affiliation start and end dates.

Directory listing. Your information is added to the Practitioner Directory. You can specify what information appears.

Community access. You gain access to the practitioner community and resources available to affiliates.

MN Mapper (formerly MNTEST) eligibility. You become eligible to apply for MN Mapper licensing if you wish to use the assessment with clients.

If your application is not approved

Most applications from trained professionals are approved. Denials are uncommon but can occur.

If your application is not approved, you will receive an explanation of the reason. Common reasons include incomplete training documentation or issues that can be addressed.

In most cases, you can reapply once the issue is resolved. There is no penalty for reapplication.

If you believe a denial was made in error, you may request reconsideration with additional information.

Re-affiliation

If you were previously affiliated but your affiliation has lapsed, you can apply for re-affiliation.

The process is similar to initial application. You complete the application form, confirm your training history, and pay the current affiliation fee.

There is no penalty period. You can re-affiliate at any time, regardless of how long your affiliation has been lapsed.

Your prior affiliation history is noted but does not affect the terms of your new affiliation.

Questions about applying

If you have questions about the application process or your eligibility, see the affiliation FAQ or contact us directly.

If you are unsure whether affiliation is right for you, review benefits of affiliation and the distinction between certification and affiliation.
